Poetry

her breath, steady and heavy
sounds of stars and celestial bodies
encapsulates my self in a boundless aura
enchanted by her beauty, her touch i adore

fleeting is the mind when i'm caught by a string
i can only submit, drifting away from reality
manifesting a mess of grey hair and dead skin
yet she accepts the soul that's buried within

blessed be the ties that brought us together
should they unravel we will find another
but while we're bound by ribbon or leather
our knots are taught strengthened by each other.
